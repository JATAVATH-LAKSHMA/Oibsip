Tribute to Someone Special

Subhash Chandra Bose

subhash chandra bose was an important leader of the indian freedom strugggle.he was called Netaji.he was elected as the president of the Indian National Congress for two consecutive terms.he was born in Cuttack,Orisssa .

Early Life

he was jailed eleven times by the British authorities.he fled india at the outbreak of World War II.he came to Germany.his aim was to win independence by fighting against the British rule in india with the help of the Axis Power.

Accomplishments

Bose authored the book 'The Indian Struggle' which covers the indian independence movement from 1920 to 1942.the book was banned by the British government.he coined the term 'Jai Hind'.
